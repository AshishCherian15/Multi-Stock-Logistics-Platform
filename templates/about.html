Help Center

Navigate to Products > Add Product. Fill in product details including code, name, price, and stock quantity. Click Save to add the product.

Go to Orders section, select pending orders, review details, and update status to 'Processing' or 'Completed' as needed.

Access Warehouse section to add, edit, or view warehouse details. Assign products to specific warehouses and track stock levels per location.

Visit Analytics or Reports section. Select report type, date range, and filters. Click Generate to view or export reports.
