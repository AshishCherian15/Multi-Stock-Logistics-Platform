Frequently Asked Questions

What is MultiStock?
MultiStock is an enterprise-grade inventory management and marketplace platform designed for modern businesses. We provide comprehensive tools for inventory tracking, supplier management, marketplace listings.
How do I create an account?
Simply click the "Register" button in the navigation bar or visit the registration page. Fill out the required information and verify your email address to get started.
What features are available in guest mode?
In guest mode, you can browse the marketplace and read forum discussions. To unlock full features like bidding, posting, and inventory management, please create a free account.
Is MultiStock free to use?
We offer a free tier with basic features. Premium plans with advanced features, analytics, and dedicated support are available for businesses with growing needs.
How secure is my data?
We use bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS), regular security audits, and follow industry best practices to ensure your data is safe and secure. All transactions are encrypted end-to-end.
